Saudi Arabia has different types of weather. In summer, the weather is very hot. The temperature can reach fifty degrees! People stay inside and drink cold water.

In winter, the weather is cool and sometimes there is rain. Children love winter because they can play outside. Some cities in the north even have cold winds.

The sun shines almost every day in Saudi Arabia. Palm trees grow everywhere. People pick dates from the palm trees in autumn. Dates are very sweet and healthy.
